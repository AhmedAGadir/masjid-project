About Us

With over 15 years of experience, Quba Education Centre aims to develop the students of today in not only a good understanding of the Quran, but also to build each individual to an excellent character and model in our society. We aim to achieve this by teaching our students core principles in studies pertaining to the Quran and also through our weekly Islamic Study plan.

These plans consist of developing a great level of understanding of key factors such as Tawhid, Messengers, Pillars of Islam and much more. Other key areas of focus will entail building on the characters of each student, which is exercised from examples set by the Messengers and people around them.
